ADJ VPAR Pak

Stewart Q., 19.05.2015
These sturdy little lights are incredible. I have bought 8 of them for our lighting rig and the light output, colour, quality and easy of use are just top notch. Fully programmable, versatile and hugely effective in the set up, these are of the finest quality and value for money on the market.

The handy soft case is ideal for carrying power/dmx leads, clamps and manuals. They take up so little space on stage and in the van, you'd hardly notice they were there.
