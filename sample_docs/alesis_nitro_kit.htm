Alesis Nitro Kit

17 ratings

Electronic Drum Set for Beginners

  • Nitro drum module with 385 sounds
  • 25 Preset Kits
  • 15 User kits
  • 60 Songs
  • Metronome
  • 6.3 mm Mono-jack outputs (L / R) for connecting to loudspeakers or mixing consoles
  • 6.3 mm Stereo headphone output
  • 3.5 mm Stereo jack Mix input for connecting an MP3 or CD player
  • 2 x 6.3 mm Stereo jack input for additional tom and cymbal pad
  • USB MIDI interface for direct connection from Mac or PC
  • Toggle switch to adjust the input of the bass drum from pad (kick pad) to switch (foot switch)

Set Configuration:

  • 1 x 08" Two-zone snare drum pad (shell / rim)
  • 3 x 08" Single-zone tom pads
  • 3 x 10" Single-zone cymbal pads (crash cymbal with choke function)
  • 1 x Kick pad including pedal
  • 1 x Hi-Hat controller
  • Drumrack
  • Cabling

Better than expected

gorkur, 09.05.2017
Bought this because I needed something small and cheap to practice on at home. I didn't really expect much from this kit but it's actually better than I'd hoped.

Rubber pads, although small (and, well, rubber) feel nice and bouncy. The cymbals feel a bit toy-ish but they get the job done. The chokeable crash is a nice feature but the ride is only single zone and, while possible, getting it to play a bell sound is tricky.

The kick pad looks big enough to support a double kicker but it really isn't since it only gets triggered when you hit it dead center, so keep that in mind. It does work fine with the Yamaha KP65 kick pad though.

The rack is sturdy enough and the kit is pretty stable while playing.

Overall, great for practicing at home and as a beginner kit.
