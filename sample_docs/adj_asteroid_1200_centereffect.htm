ADJ Asteroid 1200 Centereffect

1 Rating

LED Moving Light

  • Central beam effect with a total of 180 W RGBW LEDs (4-in-1), and infinite pan and tilt rotation
  • Advanced lens optics eliminates RGBW shadows
  • Beam angle of 4 degrees for precise beams of light
  • Pixel mappable via DMX, Artnet or KlingNet
  • 12x 15 W RGBW (4-in-1) LEDs (up to 50,000 hours)
  • Lux: 39,749 max.; 9056 Full on @ 2 M
  • Beam angle: 4°, 11° (Field angle)
  • Operational modes: Artnet/KlingNet, DMX-512, Sound Active, AutoRun, Stand Alone mode or Master/Slave
  • 3 DMX modes: 18, 20 and 64 DMX channels
  • 3- and 5-pin DMX in- and outputs
  • Stroboscope frequency: 1 Hz - 20 kHz
  • Electronic dimming: 0 - 100%
  • Power connections: Power twist input and output
  • Connections in a series: 5 Devices max. @ 110 V and 11 devices max. @ 240 V; Artnet / KlingNet: 40 devices max.
  • Power consumption: 223 W max. / 2.05 A @ 110 V, 1.02 A @ 220 V
  • Automatic switched-mode power supply: 100-240 V - 50/60 Hz
  • Weight: 22 kg
  • Dimensions (LxWxH): 400 x 450 x 600 mm

Asteroid 1200

DaleC, 02.08.2017
The Asteroid 1200 is a sturdy unit and the build quality is good. LED control panel is easy to use with a good response to touch. The unit is quite heavy, so definitely needs to be in a flight case (Unfortunately not available through Thomann). The beams are strong and provides a good light display. The motor noise level is a little high hence the low score for this category.
